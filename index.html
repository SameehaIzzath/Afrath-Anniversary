The letter
My dearest Afrath,
written a little late, but meant every single day since

I am just crazy about you. Throughout all the heavy storms in my life, I have always just wanted a gentle breeze to comfort and hold me close. I find myself craving true care, gentleness, attention, and all the warmth and happiness I feel I've missed out on for so long.

We don't know what the future holds for us, but more than anything, I want you by my side through it all. Please be my gentle breeze, my safe place to run to, and the best friend I can talk to about absolutely anything.

I always wished my husband would be all ears to what I speak, would care about my feelings, would think of me before I could think of myself — a protector who protects my heart without ever leaving a wound.

I always wished my husband would do the very little things without being asked. I love being surprised, because no one else is ever going to do those things for us, other than us.

I loved your ring. It made me feel so special — even more special when you said you'd wanted to gift it to me yourself. I loved it, da.

Happy belated anniversary, my love. Late by date, never by feeling.

forever yours
always
